Landmark Achievement

The "Impossible" Qatar Renegotiation

When no renegotiation clause existed in a 7.5 MMTPA long-term LNG contract with RasGas Qatar, Prabhat led the world's first successful renegotiation of its kind — saving India ₹8,000 crores. The PM cited it from the Red Fort. Asian LNG buyers followed suit.

💰 ₹8,000 Cr saved 📅 Nov–Dec 2015 🏆 PM-Lauded
Concept

Consumer's Model of Long-Term LNG

Proposed indexing long-term LNG contracts to daily spot markers (JKM) rather than oil — a complete paradigm shift now followed by global LNG experts worldwide.

🌍 Global Impact
Innovation

Small Scale LNG (SSLNG)

Conceptualised using LNG as liquid fuel for long-haul trucks and inter-city buses — a potential 8–9 MMTPA market (~40% of India's total LNG imports).

🚛 8–9 MMTPA Potential
Policy

Gas Pooling for Power & Fertiliser

Pioneered the concept of pooling cheap domestic gas with high-priced LNG to create a levelised price — reviving stranded power plants and fertiliser factories. Successfully implemented by the Government of India.

🏭 Govt. of India implemented
Market Innovation

Buyers Becoming Optimisers

Introduced a new tier in global LNG supply chains — the OPTIMISER — where buying companies trade cargoes for portfolio optimisation. GAIL was the world's first. Petrobras, IOC, JERA and others followed.

🔄 Global Standard
Infrastructure

Dabhol Terminal Revival

Transformed Enron's stranded Dabhol asset into a thriving 5 MMTPA LNG regas terminal at Ratnagiri, saving investors from bankruptcy and giving GAIL its dedicated terminal.

🏗️ 5 MMTPA Terminal
